Who am I?

Hey — I'm a 15-year-old cybersecurity enthusiast and web developer with a serious passion for understanding how systems break. What started as curiosity turned into hands-on experience across pentesting, networking, and creative web development.

My main field is cybersecurity — from Nmap recon to Metasploit exploits, from sniffing packets to OSINT with tools like GraphSpy and Shannon. I do all of this safely through virtual machines that let me explore freely without any system risk.

I'm also deep into AI model fine-tuning on Hugging Face — training and deploying specialized models. My next project? A pentesting AI model designed to assist with vulnerability analysis — dropping on Hugging Face soon.
